<p>Kayla Morgan, Shepherd Sophomore and member of the Middle of the Mitt High School Figure Skating Team, was a state finalist at the Michigan High School Figure Skating Championships that were held this past weekend in Novi. Kayla placed 2nd and received a silver medal in her qualifying round earning her way to the finals where she placed 7th.</p>
<p></p>
<p>We continue to get more news of wonderful things happening with our Bluejays!! Way to go Kayla. We are all very proud of your accomplishments.</p>
<p>Go Blue!!</p>
